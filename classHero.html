Protected Member Functions

void levelUp ()
 levelUp() method is used by in the deliverHit() method. When a units experience is above or equal to 100, it is called - how many times is dependant on experience, if it has N times 100 experience it is called N times. Sets a units maximumHP and damage to 1.1 times the current value. Also it sets current healthPoint to maximumHP.
