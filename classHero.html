Protected Member Functions

void lvlUp ()
 lvlUp() method is used by in the deliverHit() method. When a units XP is above or equal to 100, it is called - how many times is dependant on XP, if it has N times 100 XP it is called N times. Sets a units maximumHP and ATK to 1.1 times the current value. Also it sets current HP to maximumHP.
